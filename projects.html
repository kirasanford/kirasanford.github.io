Pip

view code

An entreprenuership endeavor dedicated to reducing produce waste at Nogales Arizona Port of Entry. The business model uses rejected- but edible- produce to create jams, jellies, marmalades, and salsas at community events to allow a longer shelf life, distribution at larger ranges, and community engagement.

Singing Plant

view code

An Arduino project aimed towards creating a plant that sings for water and light. The speaker makes various musical noises based on what the moisture and light sensors detect: whether too much or too little water or light. The speaker also makes a noise when the plant has gotten enough sunlight.
